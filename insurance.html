GOOD SAM TRAVEL MEDICAL INSURANCE

The Most Comprehensive RV Insurance Designed For RVers, by RVers!

Good Sam is proud to offer emergency hospital and medical travel insurance to our members. Since medical emergencies can be very expensive while travelling, we've partnered with TIC Travel Insurance to ensure our members have the coverage they need. All US members visiting Canada and all Canadian members visiting the USA are eligible for a two-day multi-trip travel medical insurance plan. Simply call 1-888-237-1177 to enroll or to purchase additional coverage, if needed. Get going and get covered!
